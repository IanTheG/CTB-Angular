Connecting The Beads

Rediscover the Rosary with Scripture and Artwork

This simple and elegant web app is designed to help newcomers and daily devotees pray the Rosary. Relevant scripture verses and emotive artwork for each mystery allows you to stay focused and meditate on the lives of Jesus and Mary.

You can install Connecting The Beads on any smartphone or tablet by pressing "Add to Home Screen", or shrink your desktop browser to view the app. Requires at least iOS 12.2 or Chrome 51. Built using Angular.
