Connecting The Beads

Rediscover the Rosary with Scripture and Meditation

This simple and elegant web app is designed to help newcomers and daily devotees pray the Rosary by allowing them to enter a deeper meditation on the lives of Jesus and Mary using relevant scripture verses and emotive images by the Italian artist, Bellazzi, for each Mystery.

You can view Connecting The Beads (in portrait orientation) on any smartphone or tablet running at least iOS 12.2 or Chrome 51, or shrink your desktop browser to view the app. Built using Angular.
